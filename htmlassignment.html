About This Page

This page is built using only HTML5 to demonstrate how to apply semantic elements, accessibility practices, and SEO techniques.

Accessibility Matters

Semantic HTML5 helps screen readers and other assistive technologies better understand your content. Use headings in order, label navigation, and use landmarks like <main>, <header>, and <footer>.

SEO Basics

Use Descriptive Titles and Metadata

Ensure your <title> and <meta name="description"> accurately describe your page for search engines and social media.

Structure Content with Headings

Use <h1> for the main title and descend in order through <h2>, <h3>, etc., to help both readers and crawlers navigate content.
